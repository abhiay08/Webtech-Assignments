WHEN IT IS CELEBRATED

On this day, puja is performed for ASTRA. You must offer prayers to Lord Ram, Lakshman, Bharat and Shatrughan.

In two bowls, keep coins in one and roli, chawal, flowers and dry dates (Chuara). Offer banana, radish, gwarphalli, jaggery and rice. Light diya, dhoop and incense sticks.
